Busan

South Korea's second-largest city is famous for its beaches, mountains, and temples. Don't miss Haeundae Beach, Gamcheon Culture Village, and the bustling Jagalchi Fish Market.

Jeju Island

A volcanic island known for its natural wonders, Jeju features Hallasan Mountain, Manjanggul Lava Tube, and beautiful waterfalls. It's a popular honeymoon destination.

Gyeongju

Known as "the museum without walls," this ancient capital of the Silla Kingdom is filled with historical treasures like Bulguksa Temple and the Cheomseongdae Observatory.

Korean DMZ

The Demilitarized Zone between North and South Korea offers a unique glimpse into the divided Korean peninsula. Key sites include the Joint Security Area (JSA) and the Third Infiltration Tunnel.
